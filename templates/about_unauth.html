About Our Complaint System

Overview

Our website is designed to facilitate the submission of complaints regarding university-related issues by both students and teachers. The system includes a handler and an admin panel to manage the backend operations. The proctorial board is responsible for reviewing the complaints, providing solutions, and imposing appropriate actions for any offensive behavior by students or faculty members.

Features

  • Complaint Submission: Students and teachers can easily submit complaints through our user-friendly interface.
  • Handler and Admin Panel: The handler and admin panel enable efficient management of the system's backend processes.
  • Proctorial Board Review: The proctorial board carefully evaluates each complaint, aiming to resolve issues and take disciplinary action when necessary.

Benefits

  • Enhanced Communication: Students and teachers can effectively communicate their concerns to the university administration.
  • Prompt Resolutions: The system promotes timely resolutions of issues, ensuring a conducive learning and working environment.
  • Accountability: Offensive behavior is addressed and appropriate actions are taken to maintain discipline within the university community.
